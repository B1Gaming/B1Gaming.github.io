About this mod:
Mario's New Adventure started its development all the way back in 2018.
Some of the content was already decided on day one and almost never changed during development while other stuff got changed pretty frequently.
The world themes were especially difficult to choose. Some levels were started but deleted immediately since I didn't like the theme on several occasions.
Said levels and world themes can be found in a spreadsheet linked at the bottom of this page.
