Mario's New Adventure's development started all the way back in 2018.
This modification of New Super Mario Bros. Wii aims to give its players a unique yet Nintendo-like experience.
An exciting journey with 8 worlds and more than 70 new levels awaits. Many collectibles and secrets can be found along the way.
